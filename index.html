Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) — Core Formula

We use the standard DCF formula:

$$\text{PV} = \sum_{t=1}^{N} \dfrac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} + \dfrac{TV}{(1+r)^N}$$

Where \(TV = \dfrac{CF_{N+1}}{r - g}\)
